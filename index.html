About

Toby

Meet Toby! He is a 5 year old Domestic Shorthair. He has black and white fur with pink paws. Some people say the black fur on his head looks like curtain bangs. He is big in size but he has a chiseled face like a handsome man!

Toby is a scaredy cat that also LOVES to go outside. Whenever he goes out, he doesn't come back for a while but he always finds his way home. Sometimes, he may bring home a mouse he's caught for the day. He loves to show his family the mouse he proudly caught but we would prefer if he keeps it outside!

Stewie

Meet Stewie! He is a 6 month British Shorthair. He has silvershaded fur with black paws. His fur used to be lighter but it darkens as he ages. His fur is very soft and makes him look very round.

Stewie is very food motivated and loves to eat anything and everything! He will dig throught the trash for any food he can get. He meows like he has been starved but his food bowl is always filled!

How Much Do I Feed Them?

It is important to feed your cat the right amount. You should always refer to the food label to see how much to feed your cat. I feed both Toby and Stewie 3/8 cups a day!
